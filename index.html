Ranwid's Rapid Relic Review!

A Slay The Spire-based quiz

This is a time-based quiz all about the relics from the deckbuilder roguelike Slay The Spire. You have 60 seconds to identify as many relics as possible based on images, lore and descriptions, with each correct answer adding a few seconds onto the clock. There are close to 200 relics in the game, so this is no simple task! Go out there and prove your worth as an ascender!

(Disclaimer: No explosive potions were fed to Ranwid during the making of this quiz. All relics seen in the quiz are sourced from ethical loop mode relic hoarding.)
